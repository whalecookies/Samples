Wellness at the Monterey Institute

At the Monterey Institute of International Studies, the health and wellbeing of our students is of the utmost importance.  While MIIS does not have on campus health facilities or fitness facilities, we are dedicated to helping our students find the resources they need to maintain a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

Students attending MIIS are required to provide proof of health insurance. There are a couple of options available to help you do this. One, you can enroll in our Student Health Insurance Plan or, if you already have health insurance, or prefer to find your own coverage plan, you can opt-out of this plan and provide the details of your current insurance provider. For further information read through the Health Insurance page on the website.

Please note that the information provided in these pages is not intended to diagnose, treat, or provide an opinion on any health issue.

This information is not a substitute for medical advice from a licensed medical professional. It is provided by the Office of Student Services to make you aware of the various health issues that affect students on a daily basis. Should you need specific medical advice please contact the appropriate medical officials.  You can obtain this information through the Wells Fargo Student Health Insurance Plan or through your private health insurance provider.
